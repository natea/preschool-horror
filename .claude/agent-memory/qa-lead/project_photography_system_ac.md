29 acceptance criteria (AC-PHOTO-01 through AC-PHOTO-29) drafted for the Photography System on 2026-04-12. Criteria cover: camera raise/lower, zoom, shutter, flash, film limit, anomaly lock, photo scoring, evidence grading, photo preview, and edge cases (Night 7 no-camera, all anomalies in frame, monster kill during preview, force-lower on sprint, adjacent-room photography, multi-monster flash).

**Why:** Photography System GDD exists but all sections below Player Fantasy are "[To be designed]". Acceptance criteria were authored from the user's direct mechanic spec rather than the GDD, pending GDD completion.

**How to apply:** Before this system enters a sprint, check whether the GDD Detailed Rules section has been written. If not, the AC are the only specification source — treat them as authoritative until the GDD catches up. If the GDD is filled in and contradicts an AC, flag the discrepancy to the designer.

**Open design ambiguity (unresolved as of 2026-04-12):** AC-PHOTO-10 — what happens when the player presses shutter while flash is recharging? Three options: fully blocked, fires without flash (consuming film), or fires without flash only with a toggle. Designer must resolve this before implementation begins.
